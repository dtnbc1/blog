Symbols of the land of the rising sun

Among the documents I gathered about Japan, there are a total of nine symbols representing this country. In which, the gate of heaven and Mount Fuji are two things that make me very impressed.

Phu Si Mountain

LMount Fuji is one of the "Three Sacred Mountains" (三霊山, San reizan, "three sacred mountains") of Japan along with Mount Haku and Mount Tate. Mount Fuji is a special scenic spot and a historical site of Japan. The mountain was added to the UNESCO World Heritage list on June 22, 2013 for its cultural value.

Heaven's Gate tori

According to the Japanese concept, the torii gate is the entrance to sacred places. This is considered the most genuine way to visit the spiritual gods of the "land of cherry blossoms". Therefore, before going under the Japanese heaven gate, you are required to wash your hands thoroughly and rinse your mouth to show purity. This is considered an act of reverence for the gods before entering the temple to pray.
